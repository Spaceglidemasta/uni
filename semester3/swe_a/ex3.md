### 2

The <\<include>> relationship in UML use case diagrams ressembels a dependency between one object and another.

#### Example:
Accessing your bank account on an ATM ----<\<include>>---> Knowing your PIN

### 3

The <\<extend>> relationship in UML use case diagrams adds an optional extra behavior to a use case.

#### Example:
Sleeping more ----<\<extend>>----> more energy

This does not mean that "more energy" is dependent on "Sleeping more".
